About Us

Get to know the founders behind ClassUp, what drives and motiviates us

How did we come up with ClassUp in the first place?

It all started when our founders, who were classmates at the time, were talking about how back in Secondary school, there was so much time being wasted in the classroom for most of their lessons.

They then decided to sit down at a random cafe around Haji Lane to discuss the idea of a website that utilizes Artificial Intelligence to decrease the time wasted in school. And that's how ClssUp was born!

Here at ClassUp we prioritize the users as well as our team! We aim to help students get more out of their time spent in school and help teachers waste less time in each class.

  • Everyone in ClassUp are all certified in AI Ethics and Governance
  • ClassUp is backed by the Ministry of Education of Singpaore
  • ClassUp investors are all vetted and handpicked by our founders

We make sure to hire anyone whos goals and visions are aligned with ours. As we as a team, want to make the worl, especially the education world an even better place for everyone. This has been a personal mission of our founders since the beginning and still is the mission for them. They devoted alot of time and resources into devleoping ClassUp and are still working on new features to help students and teachers in and outside the classroom. Be sure to stay tuned!
